A survey on visualization methods for distribution evolution

What is this website?

The goal of this website is to compare multiple methods of visualization of evolution of distribution over time.

How to vote?

To begin, you will need to answer a few demographic questions.

Then you will quickly be presented with different visualization methods.

Finally you will be asked to compare different visualization methods on a task. For each pair of methods, you will choose the one you prefer to complete the task or if they are similar.
