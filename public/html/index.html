Evolution of distribution visualization method survey

What is this website?

The goal of this website is to compare multiple methods of visualization of evolution of distribution over time.

How to vote?

To begin, you will need to answer a few demographic questions first.

Then you will quickly be presented with different visualization methods.

Finally you will be asked a question/task and for each pair of methods, choose the one you prefer to answer the question/task.
